About

Nike, Inc. is a multinational corporation that designs, develops, manufactures, and sells footwear, apparel, equipment, and accessories. Headquartered in Beaverton, Oregon, USA, it is one of the world's largest and most recognized sportswear brands.

Nike, Inc. is a global leader in sportswear and athletic equipment, known for its innovation, marketing, and influence in sports and fashion. The company was founded in 1964 as Blue Ribbon Sports by Phil Knight and Bill Bowerman and officially became Nike, Inc. in 1971. It is headquartered in Beaverton, Oregon, USA.

Nike Shoes FAQ

What are Nike shoes made of?
Nike shoes are made from a variety of materials, including synthetic leather, mesh, rubber, and foam. The materials used depend on the specific model and its intended use (e.g., running, basketball, or casual wear).
How do I clean my Nike shoes?
To clean your Nike shoes, use a soft brush or cloth, mild detergent, and warm water. Gently scrub the surface, avoid submerging them in water, and let them air dry. Do not use a washing machine or dryer.
Are Nike shoes true to size?
Most Nike shoes are true to size, but it’s always a good idea to check the sizing guide for the specific model you’re interested in. Some styles, like running or basketball shoes, may fit differently.
What is Nike's return policy for shoes?
Nike offers a 30-day return policy for unworn and undamaged shoes. You can return them with the original receipt or proof of purchase for a full refund or exchange.
Do Nike shoes come with a warranty?
Nike shoes come with a limited warranty against manufacturing defects. However, the warranty does not cover normal wear and tear or damage caused by misuse.
